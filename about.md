## About
This mod improves RobTop's social media UI on the main menu by moving all of Rob's social media buttons into a special pop-up, and even adding some more useful links.

---

### Use?
You can now <cy>stop misclicking those heckin' buttons</c>! Open the pop-up by <cy>pressing the new *![RobTop](frame:robtoplogo_small.png?scale=0.375)* button</c> in that same corner. Pressing each social button in the pop-up will also open <cg>a confirmation pop-up</c> asking if you want to proceed.
